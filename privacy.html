Privacy Policy

Effective July 21, 2026

Elapti is published by Ocoee Studios LLC. This policy explains how Elapti handles information when you use the app.

Current privacy posture: Elapti is designed without user accounts, advertising, analytics, or an Ocoee Studios backend. Memory content is stored locally on your device.

Information you create

You may add titles, dates, notes, categories, collections, photos, videos, voice recordings, location descriptions, coordinates, favorites, privacy settings, and memory-capsule details. This content is used to provide the app’s features and is stored locally on your device.

Device permissions

Elapti may request access to:

  • Photos and videos so you can attach media to a memory.
  • Microphone so you can record a voice memory.
  • Location so you can attach your current location to a memory.
  • Notifications so the app can provide milestone and “On This Day” reminders.
  • Face ID or device authentication so you can protect access to the app.

You can control these permissions in iOS Settings. Elapti does not receive or store your Face ID biometric data; authentication is handled by Apple’s system frameworks.

Data collection and tracking

Ocoee Studios does not currently collect Elapti memory content, location data, photos, videos, audio recordings, identifiers, usage analytics, or advertising data from the app. Elapti does not use data for cross-app tracking or targeted advertising.

Sharing

When you choose to share a memory, iOS may present Apple’s share sheet. Any destination you select is governed by that destination’s own privacy practices. Ocoee Studios does not control third-party apps or services you choose to use.

Purchases

If Elapti offers an in-app purchase, payment processing and purchase history are handled by Apple. Ocoee Studios does not receive your full payment-card information.

Data retention and deletion

Your content remains on your device until you remove it or delete the app. Deleting Elapti from your device removes its local app container, subject to any device or Apple backup settings you control. See Privacy Choices for more information.

Children

Elapti is not directed to children under 13, and Ocoee Studios does not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

Security

Elapti uses iOS-provided storage and authentication capabilities. No method of electronic storage is guaranteed to be completely secure, so you should maintain appropriate device security and backups.

Changes

We may update this policy as Elapti evolves. The effective date above will be revised when material changes are made.
