Privacy Choices

Effective July 21, 2026

Elapti is designed to store memory content locally on your device and does not currently use advertising, analytics, user accounts, or an Ocoee Studios backend.

Control app permissions

In the iOS Settings app, select Elapti to control access to Photos, Microphone, Location, Notifications, and Face ID or device authentication.

Delete local app data

To remove all Elapti data stored in the app’s local container, delete Elapti from your device. Device or Apple backups are controlled through your Apple settings and may require separate management.

Shared content

If you use Apple’s share sheet to send content to another app or person, you must manage or delete that shared copy through the destination you selected.

Support communications

If you email support, Ocoee Studios receives the information you voluntarily include in that message. You may request deletion of a support correspondence by emailing support@ocoeestudios.com. We may retain limited records where required for security, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

No sale or targeted advertising

Ocoee Studios does not sell Elapti user data and does not use Elapti data for targeted advertising or cross-app tracking.

Questions

Contact Ocoee Studios LLC at support@ocoeestudios.com.
