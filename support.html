Elapti Support

Help with memories, permissions, privacy, purchases, and app access.

Contact support

Email support@ocoeestudios.com.

Please include your iPhone model, iOS version, Elapti version, a description of what happened, and screenshots when appropriate. Do not email private memory content unless it is necessary for your request.

Common questions

How do I control permissions?

Open the iOS Settings app, find Elapti, and review access to Photos, Microphone, Location, Notifications, and Face ID or device authentication.

I enabled Face ID and cannot get back in

Use the “Unlock with Face ID” button and complete the system authentication prompt. On a physical device, confirm Face ID is enrolled and allowed for Elapti in iOS Settings.

Where are my memories stored?

Elapti is currently designed to store memory content locally on your device. Deleting the app may remove local content, subject to device and Apple backup settings.

How do I report a problem?

Email support with the steps that caused the issue, what you expected, what happened instead, and any screenshot or crash message.

Purchases and refunds

Apple processes App Store purchases and refunds. Purchase availability, restoration, billing, and refunds are governed through the App Store and your Apple account.
